From an early age I have had a distinct passion for onion tasting. I would wake in the middle of the night with a desire to indulge in the sweet bliss that was a fresh onion. My parents saw my obvious talent as an onion taster and immediately enrolled my in one of the most prestigious onion tasting schools within the country, Diverse Educational Land in Concise Innovative Onion Urology Science, more commonly going by the acronym DELICIOUS. My learning there was strenuous and intense, but with plenty of studying I was able to graduate at the top of my class of 6,000, earning honors in Onionology. During my tenure at DELICIOUS I joined the youth national team in onion tasting, I exceeded the competition earning gold in all events, including the prestigious onion throwing competition; all of my tireless efforts culminated themselves when I won the coveted Onion International Cup . As I grew up this passion did not evanesse, but instead this passion swelled, I knew this was my calling. Straight out of highschool due to my many onion tasting accolades I was drafted first pick by UCR to join their onion tasting team. This has been the experience of a lifetime and one I wish more people had the opportunity to indulge in. The nuance of an onion is something that is not typically fully understood by the uneducated plebeian mind. The onion has long been limited to a small elite, I plan to change this. I hope to take onion tasting down from the pedestal it has been placed upon, not to degrade the sport I have come to love, but instead to share my passion with a world that has not known the true bliss of a perfect onion.
